Crystal Collector: The Gempire Strikes Back

Game Rules


  1. You will be assigned a random target number at the beginning of the game."Target Number"
  2. You have four crystals ((game resources).
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Each crystal is assigned a secret number. That number will be added to your total each time you click the crystal
  4. When your total is equal to the target number, you win.
  5. If you go past the target number, you lose.
  6. Figure out the number asscoiated with each crystal. Use them with discretion!.
  7. The target number and the numbers associated with each crystal will change each time you start a new game.
